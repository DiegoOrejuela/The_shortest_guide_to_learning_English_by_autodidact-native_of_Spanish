My experience

I will share a bit of my learning process.

I am someone who, in most cases, to start something has to have clear concepts. It is an imperative for me. So for about two weeks I devoted myself to understand language as an abstract object.

I consulted about the meaning of language and its components, and delved into the area that would allow me to improve or learn a language taking into account my abilities. I think that without some knowledge that I learned in my Spanish class at school, this process would have been more difficult.
